Everything You Need to Run Your Business

Comprehensive modules designed for African SMEs

Inventory Management

  • Multi-location stock tracking for accurate insights across all your branches.
  • Expiry and batch control to minimize waste and ensure product quality.
  • Efficient barcode scanning for faster stock in/out processes.
  • Detailed stock movement logs for complete audit trails.
  • Various inventory valuation methods (FIFO, LIFO, Weighted Avg.) supported.

Sales & CRM

  • Comprehensive customer database to manage relationships effectively.
  • Streamlined quotation and sales order management.
  • Automated ETIMS-compliant invoice generation for seamless tax reporting.
  • Intuitive sales dashboards for real-time performance monitoring.
  • Automated payment follow-ups to optimize cash flow.

Procurement

  • Centralized supplier management for better vendor relationships.
  • Efficient Local Purchase Orders (LPOs) and order tracking.
  • Configurable purchase approval workflows.
  • Automated Goods Received Notes (GRNs) for accurate stock intake.
  • Detailed cost comparison reports for informed purchasing decisions.

Finance & Accounting

  • Customizable chart of accounts for robust financial structuring.
  • Easy expense tracking and categorization.
  • Automated receivables and payables management.
  • Comprehensive monthly financial reports (P&L, Balance Sheet).
  • Bank & M-Pesa reconciliation-ready for streamlined finance.

Human Resources

  • Detailed employee profiles with essential information.
  • Defined departmental roles and responsibilities.
  • Simplified leave management and tracking.
  • Basic payroll module for effortless salary processing.
  • Monthly HR activity reports for strategic insights.

Compliance & Integrations

  • Full ETIMS compliance ensures you meet all regulatory requirements.
  • Future-ready for seamless integration with other essential APIs.
  • Robust Payment & Logistics API Integrations for expanded functionality.
